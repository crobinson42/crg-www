Our Services

We are here for our customers and ready to provide excellent quality work and customer service. No project is too big or small, we come prepared for anything. Read about our services or call us to talk about your next home project!

Remodel

We are experienced and ready to take on any home remodel. If your remodel project includes the whole house or just one room, we can tear down to the studs and put it back together beautiful!

Painting & Drywall

Nothing says awesome like replacing drywall with a new textured surface and painting it with a great color scheme. We are the guys for the job - you pick the colors and texture and we paint it to perfection.

Renovation & Repair

The housing market is ripe for bringing your home up to date with a fresh renovation or simply just repairing old areas. Whether your goal is to renovate to sell or just to make things new again, we can help with that.

Building & Additions

Building a new house or structure takes planning, preparation, and experience... We're here for you! Room additions? No problem! We will walk you through the entire process from start to get the project to finish.

Roofing

The winter season does it's damage, time also wears down on the shingles (and we're not talking about a man's bald spot!). When your roof needs some TLC, give us a call and let's talk about your options.

Siding

LP siding, wood shingle, or bat'n 'board - we can help you design and pick the ride style that will make you happy. Replacing or repairing siding is season dependent and the schedule is tight, so call sooner than later!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical time for a kitchen remodel?

Kitchen remodels vary from 2 weeks up to 2 months depending on the complexity of design, how much is changing, and the size of the kitchen.

What is the average time for a bathroom remodel?

A master bath remodel typically takes about 3 weeks. Hall baths are usually 2-3 weeks because they tend to not have as much design and "fancy" features.

Can you remodel during the winter months?

Yes! The biggest obstacle for a home remodel in the winter is partitioning your living space from the area being remodeled or repaired.

Can you replace siding or do outside projects in the winter?

Yes! Of course this is weather dependent, we are willing and able to complete most exterior projects during winter months, except painting of course.

Do you recommend a designer or showroom for ideas?

The local home depot and Lowe's always have great ideas for selection on materials and design. You can also google around a bit to find some really great ideas. Our personal favorite is Pinterest!

Can you come look at our house and talk to us about our ideas?

Absolutely! Call anytime and we'll set a good time to come out and chat with you, discuss ideas, go over options, and share our experiences. We want you to be excited about your project!

When can you start?

Our schedule is constantly changing. The best bet is to call ahead of time, even if you're just thinking about your project and not set on actually doing it. Let's get something on the calendar then go from there.

Can you provide references?

Yes! There is a long list of customers that are happy with the projects, remodels, and repairs we've completed. Just ask :-)
